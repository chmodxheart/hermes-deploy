# Service Inventory

This catalog is the operator-readable view of the service-first homelab inventory.

## Source Of Truth

`inventory/services.json` is the machine-readable source for automation. This document is the human-readable view derived from that inventory and should not be treated as a second source of truth. Kubernetes manifests remain owned by `external/clustertool`; LXC envelopes remain owned by Terraform; NixOS convergence remains owned by the `nixos/` flake.

Phase 3 produces evidence only. Phase 4 selects the first migration candidate and creates the migration plan with cutover, rollback, DNS, backup, and secret handling.
